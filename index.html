Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about omachrome and OM-3 colour recipes.

How do I get a colour recipe into omachrome?
Find a recipe on om-recipes.com. Most listings include a sample JPEG shot with that recipe. Save the JPEG to your iPhone, open omachrome, and import the photo. The app reads the colour recipe embedded in the JPEG's EXIF data and saves it to your library, ready to upload whenever you want.
How do I upload a recipe to my OM System OM-3?
Connect your iPhone to your OM-3 using a USB-C data cable (not a charge-only cable). Open omachrome, tap the recipe you want, and hit Upload. The recipe transfers directly to your camera. No Wi-Fi or computer required.
What USB-C cable do I need?
Any USB-C data cable works. Charge-only cables won't work, so make sure yours supports data transfer. Most cables that came with a Mac or iPad will do the job.
What is an OM-3 colour recipe?
A colour recipe is a set of in-camera processing settings (colour saturation, tone curve, contrast, and sharpness) that controls how your OM System OM-3 renders JPEG images. Most recipes also include white balance bias, though due to a camera limitation this cannot currently be imported. Recipes let you get a consistent, film-like look straight out of camera without any post-processing.
Is omachrome free?
Yes, omachrome is free to download. There's an optional Pro unlock (in-app purchase) that adds iCloud sync across your devices and lets you choose any custom mode slot and colour profile slot when uploading recipes to your camera.
Does omachrome work over Wi-Fi?
No, recipe uploads work over USB-C only. Connect your iPhone to your OM-3 with a data cable and omachrome handles the transfer directly. No Wi-Fi or internet connection is needed.
Where can I find OM-3 colour recipes?
om-recipes.com is the main community hub for OM System colour recipes. Most listings include a sample JPEG you can save to your iPhone and import directly into omachrome.
What cameras does omachrome work with?
omachrome currently supports the OM System OM-3 only. If you'd like to see support for other OM System cameras, you can submit a request via our feedback portal.
Can omachrome brick my camera?
Very unlikely. omachrome is designed to work safely with supported cameras and we've done extensive testing. That said, use the app at your own risk. We're not responsible for any camera issues, changed settings, or data loss. Make sure your camera is fully charged and your cable is securely connected before uploading.
Can I create my own colour recipes from scratch?
Not yet. omachrome currently imports recipes from existing OM-3 JPEGs. The best place to find recipes is om-recipes.com, where photographers share their colour recipes with sample JPEGs you can import directly into the app.
